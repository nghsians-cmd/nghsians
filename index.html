About Nasirabad Government High School

NGHSIANS celebrates nostalgia, school spirit, and the stories of Nasirabad Government High School — both current students and alumni.

Founded
January 1, 1967

Welcome to Nasirabad Government High School (NGHS) — one of the oldest and most respected government secondary schools in Chattogram. Its native name is নসিরাবাদ গভঃটি. হাই স্কুল, চিটাগাং, operating under the Chittagong Education Board with EIIN 104691.

Located in East Nasirabad (CDA Avenue), NGHS sits on a large urban campus (~20 acres) with science labs, a library, an auditorium, and dedicated hostels. Our faculty average age of 51 brings deep experience focused on discipline, holistic development, and excellent academic results.

Sport is central to our identity — NGHS has a long tradition of excellence in football, cricket, and hockey, claiming national championships and producing players who have represented the country.
